Hi there! I am Rayda

Aspiring UI/UX Designer

My name Rayda, An MBA holder Electrical Engineer who is passionate about good design, learning new things and Kaizen Concept. Kaizen is a Japanese concept that means continuous improvement. And that is exactly what the UX design process is about. It is an iteration process to improve the product so when we design a product, the first idea that comes to mind should not be the final one. Attentiveness to details is one of things I value and that helps me when I design my products.

Ever since I finished my Engineering degree, I got interested in a lot of things including programming and web design. After getting my MBA degree, I started focusing more on creating products and designing them. Having well-rounded background has helped me achieve this. I like to spend my free time watching shows and learning new languages. Currently I am an indie developer, designer and blogger.
